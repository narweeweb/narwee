About Narwee

Welcome to Narwee (نروي) where ideas find the support they need to grow. We’re a Bahraini platform that helps small businesses and projects connect with investors who believe in them. Whether you’re just starting or looking to scale, Narwee makes it easier to get noticed, get funded, and keep moving forward






Our Story

We started Narwee because we kept hearing the same two things: “I have a great idea, but no one will invest in me.” “I want to invest, but I don’t know where to start.” So, we built a space to bring them together. A place where business owners can share their dreams, and investors can discover real, local opportunities worth supporting.




Our Purpose

Our mission is simple: To water the roots of Bahrain’s future by helping small businesses grow and giving investors a safe, smart way to support them. We believe that when people are connected with the right tools, great things can happen.




Provided Services

Here’s what you’ll find on Narwee:
  • Verified accounts for both investors & businesses
  • A smart matching platform to connect with the right people
  • Business profiles that show off your idea clearly
  • The Bethra Score – our AI-powered trust score
  • Easy search to explore ideas or backers
  • Affordable subscriptions (with a free first month for investors!)
